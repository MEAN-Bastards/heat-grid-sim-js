Heat Grid Simulator

Read the Readme

Click on a cell to select it and add heat to that cell. (Selected cells have a red borders.) You can heat as many or as few of the cells as you like.

Each heat interval, after the heat is added, the heat spreads from each cell to its neighbor cells.

Choose the grid size; resizing will start over. 3x3 5x5 7x7 11x11
